Layouts:

- BoxLayout: Makes it possible to stack elements vertically or horizontally.

- AnchorLayout: Allows you to place any content in the corner of the screen or in the center.

- GridLayout: Organizes the content with a number of columns or a number of rows.

- StackLayout: Organizes elements in lines ? And is able to organize elements of different sizes.

- ScrowView: Allows to use the scrow when the content is bigger than the window size.

- PageLayout: Allows to switch layouts as if they were book pages.

- Other layouts: FloatLayout, RelativeLayout, ScatterLayout.
